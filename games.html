Streamer Summer

Streamer Summer is an immersive pixel-art life simulator where you step into the shoes of an aspiring online creator. Start from a modest bedroom setup with just a webcam and a dream. As you gain followers and earn donations, you can upgrade your gear — better microphone, RGB lighting, dual monitors, and even a green screen. The game features a dynamic chat system that reacts to your actions in real-time: interact with loyal fans, roast funny comments, or ignore annoying trolls. Every decision affects your mood, stream quality, and viewer count. With 90 days of summer to become viral, you'll face random events, sponsor deals, and intense streaming marathons. Will you reach 1 million subscribers before autumn arrives? The path to internet fame is waiting!

Spend your summer

How to Spend Your Summer is a hilariously addictive 2D idle clicker that celebrates the art of doing absolutely nothing. Tap your way through lazy afternoons, collecting useless summer items like inflatable flamingos, extra-large sun hats, and remote controls for everything. Upgrade your procrastination tools: from a basic hammock to a floating pool bed with built-in snack dispenser. The game features 50+ upgrades, secret achievements for extreme laziness, and a weather system that affects your motivation. Discover bizarre combinations — like building a pillow fort that generates "chill points" or training a lazy cat to bring you lemonade. With colorful 2D art and satisfying click feedback, this game turns wasting time into an art form. Can you reach maximum laziness level and unlock the legendary "Ultimate Couch Potato" trophy?

TexnoMarket simulator

TexnoMarket Simulator delivers a complete 3D retail management experience where you build your electronics empire from the ground up. Start with a small kiosk selling phone cases and chargers. As you earn profits, expand to a full-store layout featuring smartphones, laptops, gaming peripherals, and smart home devices. The game includes over 50 authentic tech products with realistic pricing and customer preferences. Manage inventory, set dynamic prices based on demand and competition, hire staff with unique skills, and run marketing campaigns. Each customer has a personality — some bargain aggressively, others need technical advice. You can even design your store layout to optimize foot traffic. With day-night cycles, weekly sales trends, and random events like new product launches or supply shortages, TexnoMarket Simulator offers deep strategic gameplay for tycoon lovers.

Coffee Rush Tycoon

Coffee Rush Tycoon is a charming low-poly management simulator that puts you in charge of your own coffee shop. Start with a single espresso machine and a small counter in a quiet neighborhood. Master the art of making perfect coffee — grind size, water temperature, milk foam, and shot timing all matter. As your reputation grows, you'll face morning rushes with demanding customers who want caramel macchiatos, oat milk lattes, and double-shot espressos. Upgrade to professional La Marzocco machines, add a pastry display, expand seating areas, and even open drive-thru windows. The game features a day-by-day progression system with financial metrics, customer satisfaction ratings, and staff management. Hire baristas with unique perks, create signature drinks, and participate in local coffee competitions. Can you turn your small café into the most beloved coffee chain in the city?

Develop Simulator

Develop Simulator puts you in the stressful yet rewarding shoes of an indie game studio founder. Balance creative passion with harsh business realities — you have limited budget, tight deadlines, and a team of eccentric developers with different personalities. Choose which game genre to develop: RPG, puzzle, platformer, or simulation. Each decision affects development time, quality, and eventual sales. Manage daily standups, crunch periods, bug fixing, and publisher negotiations. The game features a dynamic reputation system, random events like positive Steam reviews or devastating bugs discovered after launch. Hire artists, sound designers, and marketers. Can you survive the industry and create a cult classic without going bankrupt? With multiple endings and deep resource management, Develop Simulator offers endless replayability for aspiring game creators.

Color Dash!

Color Dash! is a creative physics-based puzzle platformer where paint transforms gameplay. The core mechanic is brilliantly simple: painting surfaces changes their physical properties. Blue magnetic paint attracts metallic objects, white ice paint makes everything slippery, green slime paint adds bounce elasticity, and yellow rubber paint creates trampoline effects. Navigate your rolling ball through 35 increasingly challenging levels, each introducing new paint combinations and environmental puzzles. Use red paint to activate pressure plates, purple paint to teleport, or rainbow paint to cycle through all effects. The game features vibrant 2.5D graphics, chill lo-fi soundtrack, and a level editor where you can share your creations online. With star ratings, secret paint cans to collect, and time trials, Color Dash! appeals to casual players and speedrunners alike.

Mirror Room

Mirror Room is an elegant minimalist puzzle game built on Unity 6 that challenges your observation skills. You're presented with two seemingly identical 3D rooms — but subtle differences hide in plain sight. A book might be rotated, a painting slightly askew, or a shadow cast at a wrong angle. With only 5 hand-crafted levels (and more coming), each room increases in complexity and visual density. The game features a calming mint-jelly UI with smooth animations, a star rating system based on time and hints used, and an atmospheric ambient soundtrack. Zoom, rotate the camera, and inspect every corner. Some differences require you to change lighting or move objects to reveal hidden mismatches. Mirror Room is perfect for fans of hidden object games who want a fresh 3D perspective and brain-teasing challenges.

Bombordilo Attack

Bombordilo Attack is a chaotic 3D endless runner that celebrates internet meme culture with Italian flair. Play as Bombordilo — a quirky viral animal character — as you dash through vibrant landscapes dodging absurd obstacles. Jump over flying pizzas, slide under rolling pasta wheels, and collect espresso power-ups that trigger hilarious screen effects. The game features randomly generated environments, from Roman ruins to modern TikTok studios. Each run unlocks new meme sound effects, skins (including chef hat, streamer glasses, and gladiator armor), and special abilities like "Viral Boost" that makes you invincible. With simple one-touch controls, colorful 3D graphics, and non-stop comedy, Bombordilo Attack is pure fun for casual gaming sessions. Challenge your friends to beat your high score and unlock all 20+ ridiculous achievements. Just for laughs, just for chaos!
